# ACKO Car Market Analysis Project \ud83d\ude97\ud83d\udcca

This project involves scraping car data from the **ACKO Drive** platform and performing a comprehensive **Strategic Business Analysis** of the Indian car market across multiple states.

## \ud83d\udcc2 Project Structure

*   **`AckoDrive_Scraper_Finalized.ipynb`**: The Python script/notebook used to scrape car listings, prices, and specifications from the ACKO Drive website.
*   **`Analysis On ACKO Web site.ipynb`**: A detailed data analysis notebook providing **15 Strategic Business Insights**, covering market overview, consumer buying behavior, and segment analysis.

## \ud83d\udd0d Key Analyses Covered

The analysis notebook transforms raw data into actionable business intelligence:

### 1. Market Overview
*   **Market Composition**: Body type distribution (SUV vs Hatchback vs Sedan).
*   **Inventory Share**: Analysis of brand listing volume on the ACKO platform.
*   **Regional Trends**: Availability hotspots across Maharashtra, Delhi, Karnataka, etc.

### 2. Consumer Insights
*   **Reliability Index**: Identifying brands with the highest customer ratings.
*   **Cost Structure**: "Dumbbell Plots" showing the price gap between Base and Top variants.
*   **Green Adoption**: State-wise penetration of EVs, CNG, and Hybrid vehicles.

### 3. Strategic Deep Dive
*   **ACKO Portfolio Segmentation**: Classification of inventory into Budget, Mass Market, Premium, and Luxury segments (highlighting platform premium bias).
*   **Value Matrix**: A quadrant analysis identifying "High Value" cars (Low Price, High Rating).
*   **SUV Battleground**: Who is winning the highly competitive SUV market share?
*   **Transmission Shift**: The transition from Manual to Automatic transmissions.

## \ud83d\udee0\ufe0f Tech Stack
*   **Data Collection**: Python, Selenium/BeautifulSoup (Scraping).
*   **Data Analysis**: Pandas, NumPy.
*   **Visualization**: Matplotlib, Seaborn.

*Note: This analysis is based on data available on the ACKO platform and reflects online inventory trends.*
